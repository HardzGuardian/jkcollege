About Tilak Education Society

Tilak Education Society, founded by Shri J.N. Kurup and Late Smt. Sarada Kurup, has provided affordable quality education for 39 years. Starting small in Navi Mumbai, it now runs 16 institutions across Navi Mumbai and Thane, serving 10,000+ students from kindergarten to post-graduation.

Our Vision

"To be a leading institute fostering academic excellence, professional skills, human values, and societal leadership for tomorrow's leaders."
